ABOUT US

Home to a 46-day, multi-tier, floodlit driving range powered by Toptracer Range technology. Complimented by a practice green and bunker, Sidcup Family Golf is a perfect place for golfers of all ages and abilities.


Please note: we are a cashless venue. The range closes at 10pm with last balls at 9pm.
